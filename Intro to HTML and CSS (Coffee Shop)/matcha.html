A matcha latte is a creamy beverage made with finely ground matcha green tea powder, steamed milk, and a sweetener (if desired). The matcha provides a vibrant, earthy flavor, while the steamed milk adds a smooth, velvety texture. It's a popular drink for those seeking a balance of caffeine with a more delicate, herbal taste.
